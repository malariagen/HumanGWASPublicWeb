About cookies

Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information which are downloaded to your device when you visit most websites and search engines. Cookies are sent back to the originating website on each subsequent visit or sent on to another website that recognises that cookie.

Cookies we use

This web application only uses first-party performance cookies. These cookies are used by Google Analytics to track usage patterns in the web application. The information provided to us is aggregated and anonymous, and only used to help us improve future versions by making this application easier to use and as error free as possible. For more information, see the Google Analytics privacy overview.

Disabling cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings at any time. For more information about how to do this, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to delete them, visit All About Cookies.

To opt out of being tracked by Google Analytics across all websites, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
