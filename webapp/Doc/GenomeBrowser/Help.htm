Genome browser

Actions

  • Switch to a different chromosome using the combo box in the header of the viewer.
  • Locate a particular gene on the genome using the "Find feature" box on the bottom of the viewer.
  • Zoom in and out using of of the following options:
    • the mouse scroll wheel
    • a pinch action (on touch devices)
    • the zoom slider in the header
    • the horizontal zoom buttons in the header
    NOTE: when sufficiently zoomed in, the tracks with gene and [@snp] information will be activated.
  • Scroll through the chromosome using one of the following options:
    • dragging with the mouse
    • dragging with a finger (on touch devices)
    • the scroll slider in the header
    • the position change buttons in the header
  • Click on a gene to open the gene info pop-up (when suffiently zoomed in to see genes).
  • Click on a [@snp] indicator to open a [@snp] info pop-up (when suffiently zoomed in to see [@snps]).
  • Change the vertical scale of a track by dragging the Y scale indicator on the left up and down. This can be useful if a very high GWAS signal does not fully fit in the default range.
