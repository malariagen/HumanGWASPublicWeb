[@Snp] Frequencies Map

This view displays the prevalence of a [@snp] on a per-country scale.

Actions

  • Click the 'Select a [@snp] to display on the map' to open the 'Find [@snp]' wizard.
  • Click a pie chart to open a pop-up with more information about the selected [@snp] frequency at the selected geographic location.
  • From this info pop-up, click the study link to learn more about the study(ies) that contributed data to this population.
